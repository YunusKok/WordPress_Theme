# GDPR Agreement Settings

This document outlines the correct settings for the **"GDPR for contact forms and register"** section in your theme options. Please copy and paste these exact values into the corresponding fields.

### 1. GDPR / Privacy Policy for forms
Enable/Disable GDPR or privacy Policy checkbox for contact forms, register form
* **Value to set:** `Enabled`

### 2. Content
Add GDPR / Privacy Policy content
* **Value to set:** `I have read and agree to the `
*(Note: Be sure to leave a space at the end of this sentence so it flows nicely with the link text.)*

### 3. Link Text
Add GDPR / Privacy Policy Link Text
* **Value to set:** `Privacy Policy`

### 4. Link
Add GDPR / Privacy Policy link
* **Value to set:** `https://thessnest.com/privacy-policy/`

### 5. GDPR / Privacy Policy Validation Message
Add GDPR / Privacy Policy checkbox validation message
* **Value to set:** `You need to agree with all rental policies and terms.`

---

### Preview of the Final Result:
Once applied, the checkbox on your forms will display as:
`[x] I have read and agree to the [Privacy Policy]`
*(Where "Privacy Policy" is a clickable link directing to your policy page).*
